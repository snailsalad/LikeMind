User Documentation

Welcome to LikeMind! This guide will help you get started and make the most of your campus connection experience.

1Getting Started

Download LikeMind from the App Store or Google Play and create your account to start connecting with fellow students.

  • Sign up with your email to create your profile
  • Complete your profile with a photo and bio
  • Select your interests from our curated categories
  • Set your matching preferences
Pro tip: The more interests you add, the better your matches will be! We recommend selecting at least 5-10 interests across different categories.

2Creating Your Profile

Your profile is your first impression. Make it authentic and engaging to attract like-minded friends.

  • Profile Photo: Use a clear, recent photo where your face is visible
  • About Me: Share what makes you unique (hobbies, major, fun facts)
  • Interests: Select topics you're genuinely passionate about
  • Availability: Set when you're typically free to hang out

3Finding Connections

LikeMind uses your interests and preferences to suggest compatible friends. Here's how matching works:

  • Browse suggested profiles on your home feed
  • Send a friend request to people you'd like to connect with
  • Once both users accept, you can start chatting
  • Plan meetups through the app's event feature

4Meet Anyone on Campus

LikeMind makes it easy to connect with fellow students. Here are some tips for great meetups:

  • Get to know people through chat before meeting up
  • Meet in popular campus spots like the library or student center
  • Invite mutual friends to group hangouts
  • Check out campus events together
Pro tip: The best friendships start with shared experiences! Try meeting at a campus event or study session.

Frequently Asked Questions

LikeMind is designed for building genuine connections. Our matching algorithm focuses on shared interests and compatibility for meaningful connection — not just swiping. Who knows where a great friendship might lead!

LikeMind is designed for university and college students looking to make genuine friendships on campus. Simply download the app, create a profile, and start connecting with fellow students who share your interests.

Go to your Profile settings and tap "Edit Interests." You can add or remove interests at any time. Changes will immediately affect your future matches.

LikeMind matches you with other students based on shared interests. The more interests you have in common with someone, the higher they'll appear in your suggested connections. It's all about finding people who like what you like!

Once you've matched with someone, just tap on their profile to open a chat! Not sure what to say? No worries — LikeMind has an AI chat assistant that can help you break the ice with conversation starters based on your shared interests.
