# Privacy Policy for LikeMind

Last updated: 12/13/2025

LikeMind (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information about you is collected, used, stored, and shared when you use the LikeMind mobile application (the “App”).

By using LikeMind, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect
### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you create an account and use LikeMind, we may collect the following personal information:

University email address (used for account creation and verification)

Name (first and last name)

Major

Year of graduation

Interests

Profile information you choose to provide (e.g., short bio)

This information is used to create your profile and help connect you with other users who share similar academic backgrounds or interests.

### 1.2 Camera and Photos

The App may request access to your device’s camera to allow you to:

Take and upload a profile photo

Update or replace your profile image

Photos you choose to upload are stored securely using Firebase Storage. We do not access your camera or photos without your explicit action.

### 1.3 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we may automatically collect limited technical and usage information, including:

App usage events (e.g., logins, page views, feature interactions)

Device and app performance data

This data is collected using Firebase Analytics and is used to improve app performance, usability, and user experience.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

Create and manage your LikeMind account

Verify that you are affiliated with a university

Display your profile to other users

Match you with other users based on shared interests

Enable messaging and social interactions

Improve app functionality and user experience

Monitor usage and engagement for product improvement

Ensure safety, security, and compliance with our terms

## 3. How We Store and Protect Your Information

Your data is stored using Google Firebase services, including:

Firebase Authentication – for secure login and account management

Firebase Firestore – for user profiles, social connections, and messages

Firebase Storage – for profile images

Firebase Analytics – for anonymized usage tracking

We use industry-standard security measures provided by Firebase to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

## 4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal data.

Your information may be shared only in the following cases:

With other LikeMind users, as part of normal app functionality (e.g., your name, major, interests, and profile photo)

With service providers (such as Google Firebase) that help us operate the App

When required by law, regulation, or legal process

To protect the safety and integrity of LikeMind and its users

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App’s services.

If you delete your account, your profile data and associated content will be removed or anonymized within a reasonable period, except where retention is required by law.

## 6. Your Choices and Rights

You may:

Update or edit your profile information at any time

Change your profile photo

Control whether you grant camera access through your device settings

Request account deletion

To request deletion of your account or data, contact us at [contact email].

## 7. Children’s Privacy

LikeMind is intended for university students and is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 8. Third-Party Services

LikeMind relies on third-party services (such as Firebase) that may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review Google’s Privacy Policy for more details.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
